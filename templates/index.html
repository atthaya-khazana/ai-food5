About AI Food5

What is AI Food5?

AI Food5 is an intelligent food recognition and calorie estimation application. Upload a photo of your food, and our AI model will identify the food type and estimate its calorie content using data from the FNDDS (Food and Nutrient Database for Dietary Studies).

Supported Foods

Currently, AI Food5 can recognize and estimate calories for:

  • Burgers
  • Donuts
  • Pizza
  • Fries
  • Tacos

Data Sources

Our calorie data is sourced from the FNDDS (Food and Nutrient Database for Dietary Studies) 2021-2023, which provides comprehensive nutritional information for thousands of food items.

How It Works

1. Upload a photo: Take or select a photo of your food
2. AI Recognition: Our model analyzes the image to identify the food type
3. Calorie Estimation: We calculate the estimated calories based on FNDDS data
4. View Details: Browse detailed nutritional information in the Calorie Lookup section

Disclaimer

The calorie estimates provided by AI Food5 are approximations based on average values from the FNDDS database. Actual calorie content may vary based on portion size, preparation method, and specific ingredients. Please consult a nutritionist or use a food scale for precise calorie tracking.
